Fish

Goldfish

Friendly and easy to care for, goldfish are ideal for beginners and thrive in clean freshwater aquariums.

Betta fish

Known for their vibrant colors and flowing fins, bettas are hardy fish that do best alone in calm tanks.

Guppy fish

Small, colorful, and active, guppies are peaceful fish that are easy to maintain and great for community tanks.

Angel fish

Elegant and graceful, angelfish add beauty to larger freshwater aquariums and enjoy well-planted environments.

Neon tetra

Bright and energetic, neon tetras are schooling fish that look stunning when kept in groups.

Koi fish

Large, colorful, and long-living, koi fish are prized ornamental fish that thrive in outdoor ponds and symbolize beauty and good fortune.

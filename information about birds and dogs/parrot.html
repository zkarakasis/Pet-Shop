The name parrot refers to various species of mostly arboreal birds that form the order Psittaciformes , which is divided into 3 families: Cockatoos (Cacatuidae), Psittacidae (Psittacidae) and Strigopidae . There are currently 386 classified species of parrots, belonging to 88 genera.They are found on all continents of the world, although in Europe there is no endemic species, although some species have been reported as feral in many European countries.

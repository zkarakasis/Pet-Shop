The name parrot refers to various species of mostly arboreal birds that form the order Psittaciformes , which is divided into 3 families: Cockatoos (Cacatuidae), Psittacidae (Psittacidae) and Strigopidae . There are currently 386 classified species of parrots, belonging to 88 genera.

They are found on all continents of the world, although in Europe there is no endemic species, although some species have been reported as feral in many European countries. [ 2 ] Parrots are also completely absent from the Antarctic region . In Greece they are known since the time of Alexander the Great , when for the first time he and men from his expeditionary force brought parrots. This species is called the " Alexandrian parakeet ". [ 3 ] As for the USA, there one may encounter the Carolina Parakeet ( Conuropsis carolinensis ) or the thick-billed parakeet, although these species are not endemic there.

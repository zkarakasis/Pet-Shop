The peacock or peafowl (scientific Latin: Pavo , Greek: taos ) is a bird of the pheasant family, of the order of the ornithiformes. It originates from the forests of South India . It came to Europe in antiquity and was domesticated in an unknown way. It was known in Mesopotamia ,Ancient Greece and Rome . It was believed that it was protected by the Gods and it was common to find it in temples and courtyards of wealthy houses. Today in India the peacock is a protected species.

The peacock exhibits strong sexual dimorphism , as the male has a different appearance from the female . The male is 1.25 m long . without its tail, which doubles its length. Its color is green or blue on the chest, belly and head, it has short wings that allow it limited flight and a crest on the top of the head. It has a very impressive tail with long rudder feathers with green , blue , black and orange eye-like spots, which it opens in a fan shape.

Cat Breeds

Persian

A friendly Persian mix with a soft coat and calm personality. Ideal for relaxed homes and owners who enjoy a gentle, affectionate companion.

Siamese

An intelligent and vocal cat known for its striking blue eyes. Siamese cats are very social and love interacting with their owners.

Maine coon

One of the largest domestic cat breeds, famous for its fluffy coat and friendly nature. Maine Coons are playful, gentle, and great with families.

Abyssinian

An active and curious breed with a sleek, short coat. Abyssinians love climbing, exploring, and staying close to their humans.

Birman

A calm and affectionate cat with beautiful blue eyes and silky fur. Birmans are gentle, friendly, and excellent indoor companions.

Norwegian forest cat

A strong, fluffy cat built for cold climates. Known for its thick coat and adventurous spirit, yet very loving and loyal.

American shorthair

A balanced and easygoing breed with a short coat. American Shorthairs are adaptable, friendly, and perfect for family homes.

Oriental shorthair

A sleek, elegant cat closely related to the Siamese. Highly intelligent, talkative, and deeply attached to their owners.

Turkish angora

A graceful and playful cat with a silky coat. Turkish Angoras are energetic, intelligent, and enjoy interactive play.

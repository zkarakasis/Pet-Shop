Dogs

Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a cattle herding dog breed that originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales.[1] The name Corgi is of Welsh origin, and is a compound of the words cor and ci (mutated to gi), meaning "dwarf" and "dog", respectively.

Cannis

The dog (Canis familiaris or Canis lupus familiaris) is a domesticated descendant of the gray wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was selectively bred from a population of wolves during the Late Pleistocene by hunter-gatherers.

Bulldog

The Bulldog is a British breed of dog of mastiff type. It may also be known as the English Bulldog or British Bulldog.

Shiba Inu

DACHSHUND

One of the most stubborn small dogs that crave human presence. Excellent hunters and amazing at getting into everything. They bark a lot and cry when left alone. Otherwise they are lovable and will make you laugh a lot with their strange antics and their special walk.

BASSE HOUND

The Basset Hound is a friendly dog, both with people and with other pets, and is also an excellent companion for children. Because Bassets are scent hounds, they should always be taken outside on a leash. Although they are considered lazy, they have a tendency to run after prey, so a leash is very important for their safety.

AMERICAN STAFFORDSHIRE TERRIER

The American Staffordshire Terrier is a medium-sized, muscular dog, with a wide variety of colors in its coat. The American Staffordshire Terrier is perhaps the most misunderstood breed in the world. They have earned the reputation of being ruthless dogs that attack without cause.

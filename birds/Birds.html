Birds

Budgies (European Parakeets)

Budgies (or Budgerigars) are one of the most popular domesticated parrot species and are particularly loved for their playful but at the same time sweet character. They are particularly affectionate birds in their relationship with each other.

Agapornis the Masked

Lovebirds are not nervous birds, but on the contrary they are energetic, active and full of curiosity.Lovebirds live and breed in African nature depending on their species, on the edges of forests, in palm trees, in the savannah, in areas with acacias or river banks. They are generally small birds with a length ranging from 12-17 centimeters, depending on the species. They weigh from 40-60 grams.

Crimson Red Rosella

The Red Rosella or Red Platykerk is an impressive medium-sized parrot originating from Australia, with the red color dominating its colorful plumage. It is around 36 centimeters long and its wingspan is 19 centimeters.

Cockatiel

Cockatiel parrots are medium-sized parrots that reach 32 cm and are among the most beautiful and intelligent species. They are very playful birds, they like to have various toys inside and outside the cage.

Cannary

The canary is a songbird of the ratite order and belongs to the finch family (Fringillidae). The official name of the domesticated canary is sirinus canaria domesticus.Canaries were introduced to Spain in the 15th century after the Spanish conquest of the Canary Islands.

Common Haffinch

The chaffinch (Fringillia coelebs) commonly known as the chaffinch, also known by the names of the chaffinch and the chaffinch; includes many genera and species that are found almost all over the world, except Australia.

Parrot

The name parrot refers to various species of mostly arboreal birds that form the order Psittaciformes , which is divided into 3 families: Cockatoos (Cacatuidae), Psittacidae (Psittacidae) and Strigopidae.
